Drivers

Driver roster & histories

Max Verstappen Red Bull

Three-time world champion known for aggressive racecraft and late-braking passes. Debuted in 2015 as the youngest driver ever and quickly became the dominant force in F1.

Sergio Pérez Red Bull

Experienced race-winner prized for tyre management and podium finishes. Joined Red Bull in 2021 after strong results at Racing Point and quickly became an essential teammate to Verstappen.

Lewis Hamilton Mercedes

Seven-time world champion with a record for race wins and pole positions. Hamilton began in 2007 and transformed Mercedes into the modern era’s greatest team.

George Russell Mercedes

Young British talent with a rapid rise through the junior categories. Joined Mercedes in 2022 after impressive performances at Williams and is seen as a future title contender.

Charles Leclerc Ferrari

Ferrari’s qualifying specialist with a strong record of pole positions. Leclerc arrived in F1 with Sauber in 2018 and became Ferrari’s lead driver in 2019.

Carlos Sainz Ferrari

Consistent and adaptable, Sainz brings steady points finishes and strategic thinking. He joined Ferrari in 2021 after strong seasons with McLaren and Renault.

Lando Norris McLaren

Charismatic British driver known for quick pace and clean overtakes. Norris scored his first podium in 2020 and remains McLaren’s rising star.

Oscar Piastri McLaren

Freshly arrived from Formula 2 with a reputation for calm racecraft. Piastri claimed the F2 championship in 2021 and made an impressive F1 debut.

Fernando Alonso Aston Martin

Two-time world champion with unmatched race experience and tactical genius. Alonso returned to F1 in 2021 and continues to produce standout performances.

Lance Stroll Aston Martin

Strong qualifying performer with a history of podium finishes. Stroll moved to Aston Martin in 2021 and has helped the team grow steadily.

Esteban Ocon Alpine

French driver with a tenacious style and a Grand Prix victory in his record. Ocon joined Alpine in 2020 and is a consistent points scorer for the team.

Pierre Gasly Alpine

Former AlphaTauri winner who delivers strong late-race pace. Gasly rejoined Alpine to continue building his reputation as a top midfield driver.

Alex Albon Williams

British-Thai racer who returned to F1 with Williams after a strong comeback in Formula E. Known for consistency and clutch race moments.

Logan Sargeant Williams

American rookie with a steady start in Formula 1. Sargeant earned his seat through strong junior results and brings fresh talent to Williams.

Valtteri Bottas Alfa Romeo

Veteran Finnish driver with multiple race wins and championship experience. Bottas joined Alfa Romeo after six seasons at Mercedes.

Zhou Guanyu Alfa Romeo

China’s first full-time F1 driver, admired for his patient race development. Zhou has steadily improved his performance since debuting in 2022.

Nico Hülkenberg Haas

Experienced German racer known for race craft and consistency. Hülkenberg returned to a full-time seat in 2023 after filling in at several teams.

Kevin Magnussen Haas

Hard-charging Danish driver with a never-say-die attitude. Magnussen is a strong performer in mixed conditions and known for aggressive starts.

Yuki Tsunoda AlphaTauri

Fast Japanese driver with flair and strong qualifying pace. Tsunoda made his F1 debut in 2021 and has shown rapid improvement each season.

Daniel Ricciardo AlphaTauri

Seasoned race-winner with a trademark overtaking style. Ricciardo returned to AlphaTauri to rebuild after a challenging spell at McLaren.
